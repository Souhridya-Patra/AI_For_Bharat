## RECORDING TIPS

### Equipment
- **Camera:** Smartphone (1080p minimum) or webcam
- **Microphone:** Lapel mic or USB microphone (clear audio is crucial)
- **Lighting:** Natural light or ring light (face should be well-lit)
- **Background:** Clean, professional (or use virtual background)

### Recording Setup
1. **Screen Recording:** Use OBS Studio or QuickTime
2. **Camera Recording:** Record separately for better quality
3. **Audio:** Record in quiet room, test levels first
4. **Multiple Takes:** Record 2-3 takes, use the best one

### Editing
- **Software:** DaVinci Resolve (free) or iMovie
- **Transitions:** Keep them simple and quick
- **Music:** Subtle background music (royalty-free)
- **Captions:** Add subtitles for accessibility
- **Export:** 1080p, MP4 format

### Timing Checkpoints
- 0:20 - Should be introducing problem
- 1:00 - Should be starting demo
- 2:00 - Should be showing impact
- 2:45 - Should be wrapping up
