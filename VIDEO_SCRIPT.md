## RECORDING TIPS

### Audio
- Use good microphone (clear audio is crucial)
- Record in quiet environment
- Speak clearly and enthusiastically
- Pace: Not too fast, not too slow
- Practice 3-5 times before final recording

### Video
- Good lighting (face well-lit)
- Clean background (or virtual background)
- Camera at eye level
- Professional attire
- Smile and make eye contact with camera

### Screen Recording
- Use OBS Studio or similar
- 1080p resolution minimum
- Hide unnecessary UI elements
- Smooth cursor movements
- Prepare demo in advance (no errors!)

### Editing
- Use simple transitions (fade, cut)
- Add background music (low volume)
- Include captions/subtitles
- Export in 1080p MP4
- Keep file size under 100MB
